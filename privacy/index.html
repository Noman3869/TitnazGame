Privacy Policy for Titanz Games

Last Updated: September 4, 2025

1. Introduction

Titanz Games and its websites, games, and other applications (“we,” “our,” or “us”) are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect from you through the services we provide (the "Services"), how we use and share that data, and your rights concerning your information.

Your installation, use of our Services, and/or submission of information to us constitutes your consent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to the terms of this policy, please do not use our Services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our Services. The types of information we collect are categorized as follows:

A. Data You Provide to Us Directly:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, you may provide us with personal information such as a username, and email address.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us for customer support, you may provide your name, email address, and the contents of your message.
  • Billing Information: If you make a purchase, our third-party payment processors will collect your credit card information and other billing details. We do not store your full credit card information.
  • User-Generated Content: Information you provide when you use social features like in-game chat or forums.

B. Data We Collect Automatically:

  • Device Information: Unique device ID, Internet Protocol (IP) address, device type, operating system, and other hardware/software/firmware identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our Services, such as game progress, playtime, scores, achievements, and features you interact with.
  • General Location Data: We may infer your general location (e.g., city or country) from your IP address.
  • Precise Geolocation Data: With your explicit consent, we may collect precise geolocation data to enable certain game features. You can enable or disable this at any time in your device settings.

C. Data We Collect from Third Parties:

  • Social Media: If you connect your account to a third-party service like Facebook or a game center service, we may collect information from that service, such as your public profile name and profile picture. The information we receive depends on your privacy settings with that third party.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Manage the Services: To create your account, operate our games, process transactions, and provide you with the features you use.
  • To Improve and Personalize the Services: To understand user preferences, troubleshoot issues, and enhance your gaming experience with personalized content and offers.
  • For Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and resolve your issues.
  • For Marketing and Promotions: To send you newsletters, promotional materials, and information about special offers, contests, or events. You can opt out of these communications at any time (see Section 7).
  • For Security and Fraud Prevention: To protect against cheating, crime, or fraud and to ensure the security of our Services.
  • For Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations or court orders.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We share information with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, data analysis, hosting, and customer service.
  • With Advertising Partners: We may share non-personal information (like device IDs and usage data) with advertising partners to serve you relevant ads. For more information, see the "Advertising" section below.
  • For Social Features: If you use social features, your username and gameplay statistics (like scores and achievements on leaderboards) may be visible to other players.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe it's necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Titanz Games, our users, or others.
  • In a Business Transfer: If Titanz Games is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5. Your Data Rights and How to Request Deletion

We respect your right to control your personal data. You have the right to request the deletion of the personal information we have collected about you.

To request the deletion of your data, please email us directly at: privacy@titanzgames.com

Please include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line and provide your username or other identifiers associated with your account so we can locate your data.

Process: Upon receiving your request, we will take steps to verify your identity. Once verified, we will permanently delete your personal data from our active systems and databases within a reasonable timeframe. Please note that we may be required to retain certain anonymized or aggregated data for analytical purposes, or specific information for legal, accounting, or security reasons (e.g., transaction records or fraud prevention).

Our Services may feature advertising. Ads may be delivered by our third-party advertising partners, who may use cookies and other identifiers to collect non-personal information about your device and interests to serve you targeted advertisements. This privacy policy does not cover the use of cookies by advertisers. You can learn more by visiting their privacy policies:

7. Opting Out of Marketing Communications

You may opt-out of receiving promotional emails from us by following the "unsubscribe" instructions in those emails. If you opt out, we may still send you non-promotional communications, such as those about your account or our ongoing business relations.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete that information as quickly as possible. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email provided in Section 10.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last Updated” date at the top of the policy. For any material changes, we will notify you and obtain your consent where required by law. Your continued use of our Services after any changes signifies your acceptance of the new terms.
